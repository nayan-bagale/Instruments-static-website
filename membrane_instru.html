Drums

A drum kit is a collection of drums and other percussion instruments. It is played by a single player with drumsticks. It has some feet operating pedals that control the hi-hat cymbal and the beater for the bass drum. The drum kit may be divided into four parts such as breakables(sticks, snare drum, etc), shells(bass drum, toms, etc), extensions, and hardware(drum stands, pedal, etc).
